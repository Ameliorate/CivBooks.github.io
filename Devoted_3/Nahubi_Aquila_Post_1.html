DocWifi Introduces Bill to give Landowners further rights By Nahubi On Friday night, DocWifi proposed legislature on the Aquila subreddit that would give landowners more rights under law. (cont. next page)
Page 7 of 13
The purpose of the Land Rights Act, as stated, is the following: The purpose of this bill is to define the rights of land owners within the city of Aquila and in the outlying territory, to designate powers of eviction and demolition, and to grant
Page 8 of 13
powers of eminent domain to the Government of Aquila. Of course, the Act gives the right to land owners protection from trespassing, breaking and entering, and unlawful search and seizure, as well as the right to sell their land.
Page 9 of 13
However, the Act would also give the Executive Council the ability to give land out to new residents (rather than the legislature), evict any person found guilty of a crime, and to purchase property from citizens via eminent domain for government use.
Page 10 of 13
It also makes it illegal to tax road usage, and officially gives all road blocks from infinite depth and height to the government of Aquila, along with any government building. While the majority of these rights are good, it also gives more power to the
Page 11 of 13
executive branch of government. It would also reduce the time of citizenship loss after sale of property from "until the next census" to 24 hours after the sale. MrShroom97, who campaigned with the Free Bird Party, promised small government as a principle
Page 12 of 13
of his party. However, there is no legal way for the Executive Council to veto an order like this, so his only alternative would be to convince the legislature to vote against the law.
