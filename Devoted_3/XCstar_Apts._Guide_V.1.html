Welcome to the official Public Housing Complex of Chanstantinople. We specialize in providing a foothold to players new to the town while they gain materials, armor, xp, etc., while needing a place to live.

BUILDING LOCATION The apartments is a tower complex located in downtown Chanstantinople with quick access to local amenities such as the town workshop, markets, and the transporation center.

BUILDING AMENITIES The building is reinforced with STONE, requiring 50 block breaks on a single block to actually break it. Check out the 1st floor lobby for building notifications and updates to the directory.

BUILDING AMENITIES (2) The building features a minecart elevator for quick vertical access to upper floors. Simply get in and get out to use. Use the elevator if your desired floor is 2 or less floors above or below the dropoff floor.
