I: Clouds This may sound dumb, but the clouds in this place do appear to have intentions, specially in the high peaks of this place of chaos. Fog constantly covering every hole, ready to deceive the unwary to fall to maybe an inconvinient hole.

III: Burnt. A demon taken straight from the Nether. Placed on the surface of earth to cause chaos and madness. They seem to be lonely creatures, as it's rare to find more than 1 in a same day. But they're not to be under-estimated.

IV: Ghasts. By far the rarest of creatures on this island, ever since I came to own this land, I've only seen one. But from what I can gather, they're not dangerous at all as long as you have good reflexes. They can be noticed by their muffled moaning.
