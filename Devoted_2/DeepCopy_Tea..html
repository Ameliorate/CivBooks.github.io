Keeping a stiff upper lip and brewing a proper Tea ---------------- I'm sure you've read some popular trap about teas or coffee, and how to prepare them.
Page 2 of 3
Well, they're wrong. The essence of a good tea is in the leaves you use; choose well, and use at least half a dozen per pot of tea. Like all good teas, milk is essential to the finished product.
Page 3 of 3
Ignore this, and you're left with... boiled leaves. Not tea. Don't cook it too long or the leaves will singe; a couple of minutes will do. Sit back, sip your tea, and be assured of your superiority over all.
