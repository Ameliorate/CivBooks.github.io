Civ Book Viewer

Read full books written on Civ servers.

Search

Search by Server: Civcraft 1.0, Devoted 2.0, Devoted 3.0,

Books from the following servers will be added in the future: Civcraft 2.0, CivTemp, Civcraft 3.0, CivEx 1.0, CivEx 2.0, CivEx 3.0, CivEx First Light, Devoted 1.0, CivClassic 2.0

You can search for any words in the book title. Additionally you can search for specific servers ("realms"/"ages") and authors (currently only signees):

  • :server:Civcraft_2 to only show books that were available during Civcraft 2.0,
  • :server:Civcraft to only show books that were available during any Civcraft iteration,
  • :signee:SomethingSaucy to only show books signed by SomethingSaucy.
In the future, full text search, search by topic, original author, library, time of writing, Lawa Nasin, etc. may become available.
