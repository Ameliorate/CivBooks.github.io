I. Introduction This article contains advice on how to effectively maintain leadership, such as that of a clan. This advice is cold and pragmatic, disregarding ideological values, moral values and other wishful thoughts,

describing politics as they truly are and not how we wish they were, and values one's own survival over naive do-goodery. This is not an ethics discussion, as ethics are so highly subjective it is foolish to even consider using

II. Conduct It is unwise to do good without reason, but it is more unwise to do evil if the populace will perceive it as so; when you must do evil, do it in secrecy. Do not ever stick to a set of principles or morality, you must take the action that
Page 9 of 30
has the most favourable consequences for you, in both the short and long term. The only thing that matters is your own survival, and survival is best guarded by maximizing your power and influence over others, so
Page 10 of 30
that you may crush all those who seek your death, and exploit those who would not stop your death. It is unwise to let yourself fall into the delusional and utopian morality of the meek, as you will only be trampled on, scorned by men

and praised only by god. However, neither shall you ever be wanting to fall into the short-sighted immorality of the brute, as you will only incite the slaves into rebellion, scorned both by men and god. In order to succeed, you must not follow any

principles or morals, you must be a wolf in sheep's clothing, exploit the slaves without their knowledge and turn them on their masters, praised by men and scorned by god. Judge things not by their intent nor conduct, but by their consequences;
