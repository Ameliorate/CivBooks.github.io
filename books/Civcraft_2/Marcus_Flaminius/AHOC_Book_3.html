- - - - - - - - - - - - Conclusion - - - - - - - - - - - - And so here we are today. My aim in creating this book was to present a history of the server. I wanted to show what worked and what didn't on CivRealms. It's a unique server indeed. What
Page 12 of 22
works on a factions server does not necessarily work here. The same is true of a towny server. It calls for an interesting playstyle, one that requires you to think critically and weigh the diplomatic repercussions of your actions. Unless, of
Page 13 of 22
course, nobody can really stop you. Then you can do whatever the hell you want. But such is the nature of civilization. The powerful will subdue the weak. Do not trust power. Over the course of CivRealms there has not been one benevolent
Page 14 of 22

Page 15 of 22
