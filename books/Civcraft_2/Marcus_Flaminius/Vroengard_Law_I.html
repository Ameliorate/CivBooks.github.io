II. Land as a Peasant i. Acquiring Land a. . Peasants are barred from owning any land within the lands of the Tetrarchy. They are afforded plots within the peasant housing district. Any and all structures built on
Page 12 of 48
that plot must have a member of the Tetrarchy as moderator on the group. b. In return for this land, peasants are expected to work farms, tend to animals, and gather resources from the mines and the wilds. These
Page 13 of 48
resources are then deposited in the town stores after the peasants have deducted what they need for their personal use. c. The Tetrarchy reserves the right to evict peasants for any reason.

III. Land as a Citizen i. Acquiring Land a. Citizens may purchase lands in and around Vroengard and its territories excluding land within the city. Any land grants will be handled by the City Planner and Resource Minister
Page 15 of 48
b. The Tetrarchy reserves the right to refuse any land sells. c. The Owner will retain rights to all land sold from bedrock to sky.
Page 16 of 48
ii. Selling Land a. You may sell your land to another citizen of Vroengard. Any attempt to sell or grant sole or partial ownership to someone who is not a citizen will be considered forfeiture of the land. At that point all lands will default back to
Page 17 of 48
ownership by the Tetrarchy. iii. Plots within the Capital a. Citizens have the sole right of obtaining access to plots within the Capital.
Page 18 of 48
b. Plots have a monthly rent associated with them, set by the Resource Minister. Rent is due on the 1st of each month. c. Citizens are limited to only one residential plot.
