To start off, we will look at the 3 basic laws of mining anywhere.§0 §01) "Don't dig straight down." Simply because of caverns or lava below, there is a possibility of dying.§0 §02) "Don't dig straight up." Gravel, sand, or water can fall down and suffocate or

drown you.§0 §03) "Don't dig directly infront of you." The lets known of the 3. In places (especially the nether) what is directly infront of you could kill you. Be read to block or dodge oncoming lava or hostiles.

Types of Mining:§0 §0Branch mining: 1wide, 2 high tunnels that go on for a while. Usually 2-3 blocks apart. §0 §0Pros: Covers a large area.§0 §0Cons: Possibility of missing veins.
Page 7 of 10
Strip Mining: Removing an area of stone layer after layer.§0 §0Pros: completely visability of ores, lots of stone material gained.§0 §0Cons: Extremely tool heavy, time consuming.

Cave Mining: Running through caves grabbing every ore you can.§0 §0Pros: Extremely low tool use. Fun. Already open veins.§0 §0Cons: Torches required, lots. Some ores removed upon cave generation. Not very effective in populated areas.
