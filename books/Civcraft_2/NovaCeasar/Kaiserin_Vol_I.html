The City of Kaiserin is the capitol of the Commonwealth of the Kaiserinreich, a nation located in the northern minus minus. Founded in late September of 2014, it has since grown to be a moderately large and prosperous city.
Page 3 of 31
The City of Kaiserin was founded in September of 2014 as a colony of the late Hogereich, the Kaiserinreichs predecessor with a similar governmental structure and populous.
Page 4 of 31
Founded while the Hogereich was in a state of decline, the colony of Kaiserin was initially started in the Great Northern Caverns as a mere rail station on the way to the desert territory of Deseret as a way to store goods and allow for faster trading.
Page 5 of 31
The status of Kaiserin changed however in early October when explorers found that the cavern itself was mostly flat and quite suitable for making a larger colony.
Page 6 of 31
This, coupled with the HCF invasions of around the time, made the place seem like an ideal hidden city, and a large number of resources and people began to flow into the new colony.
Page 7 of 31
Growth continued for some time as a Hogereich colony, when sometime in early November/late October the Hogereich leader NovaCeasar made contact with a northern nation called Volterra, lead by an informal Emperor (at that time), named Mickale.
Page 8 of 31
Upon contact with the Volterrans, Kaiserin, which was bordered on the north by them, became a much more funded and supported city in order to foster trade and for the first time in Hogereich history, hopefully gain a friend in the region and perhaps ally.
Page 9 of 31
With the growth of Kaiserin came the increasingly faster decline of Hogeklif, the capitol of the Hogereich. So much so that by the middle of November, it was decided that the Hogereich should be dissolved, and a new nation know as the Kaiserinreich would
