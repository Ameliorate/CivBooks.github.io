The Constitution of the Sovereign Republic of Little Latvia
Page 2 of 22
In recognition that all Steves are created free and equal by the Holy Potato and that all power flows from Him and His agent on server the Patriarch of Baznicas Sveta Kartepelu; we the people of Little Latvia establish that all are entitled to the basic
Page 3 of 22
rights of privacy, property, and the pursuit of happinesss. Throught the power vested in the office of the Patriarch we the citizens of Little Latvia endeavor to set forth in this document protection of these rights.
