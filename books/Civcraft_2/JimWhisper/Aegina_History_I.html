October After a long absence I returned to Aegina, the island jewel of the Danzilonan Federation, with the intent of settling down for good. And what a change in four months! When I first made Aegina my home there was nothing there save for a few
Page 3 of 28
wooden huts, the abodes of myself and the founders of the island, and the two main towers which already stood tall and proud. Sometime when I have the stories I shall compile a history of Aegina from its founding, but that is a labor for another book.

Yet now I find four towers where once two stood, the work of many completed by nightmaresplodey. And another pair of mighty structures, the Twins, burn brightly alongside them, a beacon of prosperity and new life for the town constructed by White_n_Pasty.

Finding the new leader SourDust2 a welcoming and benevolent monarch, I determined to once again raise a humble domicile of my own. My old house was long gone; where it stood I cannot say, but it pales in comparison to the new. I have

finished a humble oak cottage, neither large nor lavish, but rustic and filled with the comforts of home. I sought out a number of trees and sugarcane and grasses to make it as charming as possible, and SourDust2 helped by adding touches of nature as

well. Now as I write I sit beside the sea, in a large open meadow with flowers of every kind. Below me are the vast catacombs of the abandoned mineshaft that weave in and out of the low hills on the island, and they are accessible from my own back yard!

October has been a month of unprecedented growth for Aegina. From a small holding of merely a few members it has tripled or quadrupled in size, with new houses grand and small popping up every few days. We have gone from a barely active DZF territory to a
