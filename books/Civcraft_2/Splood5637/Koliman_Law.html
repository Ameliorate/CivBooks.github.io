Koliman Law 1. Avoid terraformnig if at all possible. Build around the landscape, not into it. 2. Confine any large-scale logging to the designated tree farms. 3. Always, always replant.
Page 2 of 4
4. Always harvest trees fully. Anyone found guilty of harvesting the bottom few logs of a tree and leaving the rest will be pearled. It is a crime against nature and will not be tolerated. 5. Fire and lava are strictly prohibited.
Page 3 of 4
6. No thievery or griefing. 7. Sand may only be harvested from the area surrounding Orthanc, and should be replaced with dirt to help Orthanc grow. 8. Large-scale builds should be run past the Kolima High Council before starting construction.
Page 4 of 4
9. Any non-renewable resources (diamond, coal, etc.) should be mined responsibly, using a soft-touch pick. 10. All builds must follow an eco-punk aesthetic. ≈≈≈
