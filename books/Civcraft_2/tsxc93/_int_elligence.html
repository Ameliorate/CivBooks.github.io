Neverwhere Mostly inactive town, one player is on occasionally. Massive artistic buildings reinforced with stone. Easy to break into, chests are unlocked and contain numerous building supplies. No reddit presence. (-,+)

Commonwealth Massive rail hub. Access can be given to the entrance into the town, which includes a number of snitches in the area. Stone reinforced obsidian around the rail area, vulnerable town otherwise. Massive shopping area. (-,+)

Commonwealth Cnt. Snitches include the rail system leading in and out of CW. This can be used to track movement of potential targets. Background check required to get permission, consists of reddit search of your name. Active on Reddit.
