on Civcraft_2
Page 1 of 3
Welcome to BlackCrown's state of the art library card door access system! Step 1: With TWO official BC library cards in hand, double left click on the item exchange chest by the purple access door. You will receive a "door pass."
Page 2 of 3
Step 2: Walk through the door, which will now be open. Step 3: While on the inside of the library, double left click on the item exchange chest with your door pass to retrieve your two library cards. The door will close again.
Page 3 of 3
Step 4: When you are ready to leave, the pressure plate on the inside of the library will open the purple access door again for your departing pleasure.

Part of the Civ Books project by Gjum.
