Council There will be a council that will hold the power to control the city. Its main responsibility is to oversee and plan the continued growth and development of the city whilst upholding and promoting the values on which it was founded.

Mainly, this will consist of administration and distribution of property, management of foreign affairs, and organization of defense/militia forces. Circumstance permitting, the council will strive to minimize its involvement in the operations of the

city. Basically, unless unique circumstances arise, the council will only handle the responsibilities listed above. Membership of the council will be comprised of individuals who have contributed a significant investment of time and labor to the city as

well as being fully vested in its values and long term success. The addition of council members shall be voted on by exisisting members, and thus will be inherently subjective. A voting majority will be used to pass all matters that come before the
