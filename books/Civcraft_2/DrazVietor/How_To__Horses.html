BREEDING THE BEST =================== You want the stronkest horse? Or a jumper? Fast? Test each horse for the desired stat you want. Hearts are obvious, stack blocks for jump height, and worry not about
Page 21 of 32
speed unless you are really unlucky. On Civcraft, all horses are SUPPOSED to go the same speed... but they may or may not. So check yourself. Breeding selectively means you take your two horses with the stat you desire and make them breed together.
