§lThe Story of God §r1. God is what we know as the “the Father,” “Yahweh,” “the great I AM,” and “the Lord.” 2. The infinite and eternal God exists outside of physical law as He is both the creator and sustainer of the universe.

3. God created all the world out of His love. 4. Out of all of God's creatures, God set human beings apart. 5. He gifted them with consciousness and intelligence, making them in His image. Humans sounded the heartbeat of God.
