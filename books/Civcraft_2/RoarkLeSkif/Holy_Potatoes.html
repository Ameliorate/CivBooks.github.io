Chapter 2 ##### The architecture in LL is quite different from that of Churchill. Where as Churchill has an organic feel, with winding roads and quaint houses, LL is rather square and organized. Roads lie in straight lines and gray cold buildings
Page 17 of 49
rise straight up into the air. Vines cover most of these skyscrapers to break up the monotony. That's not to say the buildings aren't impressive. Each one is bigger than the last, with multiple stories all filled with something. One's a museum,
