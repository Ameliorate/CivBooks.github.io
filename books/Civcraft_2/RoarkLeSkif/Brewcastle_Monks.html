Introduction ################### Brewcastle is a monastery hidden away in the wilderness that houses the Order of Brewcastle Monks. Brewcastle Monks live their lives devoted to two things; books and beer. These are the commandments of our order.
Page 3 of 12
Brewcastle Commandment #1 ################### Thou shall breweth that tastiest of drinks so that the world may be a far drunker place.

Brewcastle Commandment #2 ################### Thou shall consumeth only what thou cannot breweth.

Way of Life ################### Brewcastle is designed to best accomodate the needs of a brewer and a bookkeeper, with terraced farms cut into the mountainside with water flowing down onto the crops. A typical day in a monk's life might

include gathering ingredients for potions, fishing in the nearby river for Pufferfish, or even venturing to the nearby towns where they are welcome to use their farms and gather more rare ingredients. Monks rarely mine, instead relying on
