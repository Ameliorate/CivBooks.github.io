Hockey Blitz Beer ###################Our first beer ever crafted, it's name is a throwback to the days of hockey in New Danzilona, of which the Swisston Shovels were a part of. This beer exceptionally hoppy and is sure to put a spring in your step!

Melon-Aid Ale ###################A fruity beer made with fresh melons harvested outside New Danzilona, it has a nice kick that invigorates and replenishes. A good drink to cool off with after a hard day's work.
Page 5 of 10
Late Night Lager ###################Darker and a bit earthier in flavor, this lager is a nice contrast to our lighter beers. It has a lighter note from the New Leningrad carrots infused in its flavor, and it'll help keep you fresh and alert at night.
Page 6 of 10
Puffer Porter ###################Combining the traits of a pufferfish with fresh Trackistan hops we've crafted a delightful flavorful porter with minor vanilla notes. Great for a day at the beach, it can help you swim to the other shore!
Page 7 of 10
Spider Bite Stout ###################A bitter stout made with real spider venom, this beer packs a kick. Gathered from abandoned mineshafts near Swisston, the venom really adds a bite that's sure to get you twisted.
Page 8 of 10
Ringwraith Pale Ale ###################A seasonal brew from the Swisston Brewing Company and in short supply, this potion is a twist on our popular Late Night Lager with a maltier palate and a bitter finish. Let's the imbiber blend in, so to speak.
Page 9 of 10
Buff Bock ###################A raspberry Bock with grapefruit undertones, this beer will really make you feel like you can conquer the world! With an exceptionally high alcohol content this drink let's you talk smack and back it up!
Page 10 of 10
Kangaroo Kolsch ###################A lighter beer with a lighter edge, the Kangaroo Kolsch is currently being studied in our brewery. Using mild netherwart grown in the actual Nether, this beer can help you go leaps and bounds above the rest!
