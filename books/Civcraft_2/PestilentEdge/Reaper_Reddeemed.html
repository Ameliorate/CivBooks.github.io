Once upon a time, there lived a rabbit named Comrade Buuny. One day, Comrade Bunny was in the publicly owned Luxem Terrace Farms looking for carrots. All of a sudden, he sees something monstrous coming towards him!

So he asks that monstrous, evil thing; "Who are you? And why are you in Luxem's glorious gardens?!" The thing replied; "Well, my name is DiamondReaper and I don't want to harm you. The only thing I ask is if you'll be my friend."
