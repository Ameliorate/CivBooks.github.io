These are the official laws of the City of Churchill. The jursidiction of Churchill is anything within and including the buildings in which were created by citizens or by the town itself. No single member is to be exempt or above these laws.

I) Zero Tolerance to Griefing Any individual accused of griefing with malicious intent will be pearled and held until they are able to stand trial. Should they choose not to stand trial, a neutral judge will deem a sentence based on either

A) Time in The End B) Hard labour C) Reparations Any unreasonable judgements may be voted on the town via the subreddit or taken to a neutral arbitrator.

IV) No Hate Speech Any user found guilty of hate speech while be pearled and given trial. The result of the trial will be determined by the presiding judge. There are two results of this A) Reparations B) Time in the End

These laws are subject to change based on the situation. Each crime is subject to one punishment. For example, each count of griefing is subject to a cumulative punishment for each incident. All of which will be determined by the presiding judge.
