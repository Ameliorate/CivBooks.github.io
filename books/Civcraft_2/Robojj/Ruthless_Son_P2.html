Little did we know, it was. We approached the dock and put our tacklebox down and casted our poles. "I bet you 15 damn bucks you cannot catch a clownfish" Jack dared me. "Hand over the money, it's gonna be mine anyways!" "You wish" he replied.
Page 2 of 4
My pole tugged hard. No warning. "Dangit, this aint no clownfish!" I said. "I don't think it is a fish at all! Did we catch a submarine?" we laughed as I pulled it in. It came out of the water and it was orange and white. Fifteen bucks hanging off a pole.
Page 3 of 4
"I told you man" I said to Jack. "Well, you got some skill." he replied as he reached his hand out with a ten and a five. I slapped his hand and put the money in my pocket.
