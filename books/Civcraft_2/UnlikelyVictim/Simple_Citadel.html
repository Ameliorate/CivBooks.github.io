§l What is Citadel? §r Citadel is a plugin that allows players to protect their blocks and chests from other players, by consuming 'reinforcement materials'.
Page 4 of 21
§lSmoothstone §rThis is the lowest level of reinforcement. This will protect your blocks or chests for 25 breaks. §lIron Ingot §l§rThis is the middle strength reinforcement, protecting against 250 breaks.
Page 5 of 21
§lDiamond §rThis is the top level reinforcement, protecting against a huge 1800 breaks. This is recommended, among many other things, for your most valuable chests.

§lGetting Started §rBefore we start, it's important that you create a group to reinforce to. This is because you should NEVER reinforce privately, doing so will leave you unable to grant people access to your blocks or chests.
Page 7 of 21
If you're pearled want someone to hold your valuables somewhere safe? Can't you private locked your chests. Need to pay bail to escape bail for a crime you committed? Can't privately locked chest.
Page 8 of 21
To create a group we use the command /ctcreate 'name' where 'name' is your chose group name. An example would be /ctcreate Fishfood This would create a group named Fishfood ready for your use.

§lHow to Reinforce? §r Now we have our group we are ready to reinforce all our things! The main commands we will use are /ctr /ctf /ctb /cti

§l/ctb §rThis command allows you to break your own reinforcements. Use it to remove or change parts of your builds. Chests and hoppers will require two breaks to remove. The first break removes only the reinforcement material.

§l/cti §rThis command enters you into 'Info Mode'. Using this you can left click punch blocks to inspect them for reinforcements. Useful for checking the status of a reinforcement. As a reinfocement is broken it will pass through stages of
