"This book is an instruction guide that will hopefully help you get good ideas on how to be a more effective and conservative worker for our glorious community!"
Page 2 of 6
Step 1: Dedicate yourself. Log in on an often enough basis and try to put in at least a small amount of time each day into ongoing projects. Every day for 15 minutes to several hours would do wonders!
Page 3 of 6
Step 2: Keep a general idea of your how effective you are! We as a community all need to find a perfect balance of: Progress, Profit, and Loss. Do you really need a diamond pickaxe for creating a strip mine? No. But is a wooden pickaxe effective? No.
Page 4 of 6
"You see my point? If you are mining out a wall, why not go work at the tree farms, keep some of the wood, and make stone pickaxes with it? This will create 0 loss, 100% profit, and hopefully a large amount of progress! That\u0027s what I call beinge effective!"
Page 5 of 6
Step 3: Put all unneeded items in the storage room. This one is a personal step that I take, that may not be for you. Anything I do not immediately require, i put away into storage for the use of us all. Perhaps you should consider it too!

The End. The book may be over, but the fight for perfect effective working is not! Don't you want to help in the fight? :)
