Wheatland was founded on the principles of freedom to grow what one wishes. It welcomes people of all races and creeds, except niggers, who are willing to live a life in the service of The8thBit. Its journey begins, with a newfriend named FlameLegion.
Page 2 of 5
The young adventurer was travelling to see the vast "Titan Vault", after walking many miles, and crossing many oceans, our glorious leader found the promised land. He resigned from his long journey and began constructing the first tower on the coastline.
Page 3 of 5
It was not long after the original founding of this Tower, that the Couragous Nat_Born_Killer joined him. The two set about reforming the land, creating vast fields of Wheat, and building the great Castle Wheat. The two were soon joined by another.
Page 4 of 5
Honeyelf found the two and set about mining the great Dwavern Halls, during this time two others joined the community; Werebee, and Mrpip. FlameLegion then formed the first council, consisting of him, Honeyelf, and Nat_Born_Killer.
Page 5 of 5
Shortly after this the town was attacked by evil Nazi's who burnt down Mrpip's house, and filled the town with swastikas. This caused the traitor Honeyelf to leave the town and take with him the entirety of the town's wealth. Contact with Kolima began.
