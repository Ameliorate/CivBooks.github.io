Since the dawn of time, Tropica had been made up of 10 different islands. Most of them were sandy and barren. However, two stood out. One of them was the biggest island of them all, which we came to call Rosia, and the other one was a smaller jungle

island that we named Salem Island. The island was hilly and thick with the bush of the jungle. On that island, we found the only cats that inhabited all of Tropica. The first cat that we tamed belonged to my friend Milkshake, and he named it Salem. So, we

named our little island after his cat. Personally, I valued Salem Island highly. It had unique geography and housed the only other animals in Tropica that weren't sheep or chickens. Unfortunately, Milkshake did not feel the same way.

In the early days of Tropica, Milkshake built the majority of the buildings, and he was the best and most respected builder in our little group. For a while, he had wanted to build a little pub somewhere in Tropica. On February 16, 2014, he found a spot
Page 5 of 10
for it, and that spot was Salem Island. Tropica at the time was an anarcho-communist settlement. Instead of instituting laws, we all worked together and tried to consider one another when making decisions. This time, however, Milkshake did neither.
