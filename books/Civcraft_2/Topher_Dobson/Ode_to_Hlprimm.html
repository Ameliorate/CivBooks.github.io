Signed by Topher_Dobson

He who fell meters seventy and two/ Onto a carpet deceptively blue./ Lost emeralds and helmets and shovel with name,/ Into my hoppers now mine to reclaim./ He dashed back to death's spot to gather his loot,/

With panic broke through reinforcement en route./ His lesson is learned, sign posted on door:/ "PRIVATE - NO ENTRY" one must not ignore.
