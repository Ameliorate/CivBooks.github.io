***********************H-DOME Spleef Rules ***********************
Page 2 of 4
1. No placing of blocks from players or spectators when a match in is play. If a block is placed the offending player loses the match. If a spectator interferes a rematch is called. 2. Potions are not allowed.
Page 3 of 4
3. Players are not allowed to hit eachother. Three hits cause the offending player to lose the match. 4. Beginning before the announcer announces "GO" results in a restart. 5. No foreign objects
Page 4 of 4
6. Leaving the playing area results in a lost match. 7. If a player punches a another player off the playing area the offending player loses the match, even if the hit was not on purpose.
