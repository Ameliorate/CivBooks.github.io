§r §l The Constitution of the Kingdom of Gondolin §r
Page 2 of 23
Gondolin is a monarchy under the King, consisting of the City of Gondolin and outside territories. The King of Gondolin is the head of state and chief executive for the entire kingdom of Gondolin. The King serves as the representative of the kingdom in
Page 3 of 23
all foreign affairs, unless specifically delegated. The office of the King is permanent, and may only be passed on by voluntary abdication and appointment of a successor. The King acts as the sole authority within the City of Gondolin and is responsible
Page 4 of 23
for its protection and governance. The King shall be granted all rights and privileges of residency and/or citizenship in any and all territories of Gondolin.
Page 5 of 23
A territory of Gondolin is an area outside of the City of Gondolin that is recognized by the monarchy as part of the kingdom. Territories may implement local governments at the discretion of the monarchy, provided that they do not violate Gondolin law.
Page 6 of 23
The local governance of a territory, if any, is limited to the bounds of the territory as recognized by Gondolin.
Page 7 of 23
The King may, at his discretion, appoint other players as Nobles. Nobles are granted an appropriate title and responsibility for a territory, and act on the King's behalf within this jurisdiction. Within the appointed territory, the words and actions of a
Page 8 of 23
Noble are legally equivalent to those of the King, except when overruled by the King. In all other ways, Nobles act as citizens of Gondolin and are subject to its laws.
Page 9 of 23
The government of Gondolin is founded on the monarchy's interpretation of the Non-Aggression Principle and voluntary association. The monarchy derives its authority from the consent of the people in continually associating with Gondolin. The
Page 10 of 23
monarchy shall create and execute laws consistent with these ideals and the authority described herein.
