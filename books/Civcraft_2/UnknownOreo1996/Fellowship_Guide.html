Introduction ------------------- Welcome to Fellowship! We're a bustling city located at 6100, -2800 boasting over 50 citizens. Within this guide you'll be able to learn all about this wonderful town! /r/CivcraftFellowship
Page 2 of 9
Citizenship ------------------- Becoming a citizen of Fellowship is simple! All you have to do is sign the "Citizenship Thread" which can be found on /r/CivcraftFellowship. (Look under "Important Links" on the sidebar.)
Page 3 of 9
Getting a Home! ------------------- To acquire a home within Fellowship all you have to do is ask a government member or Fellow, they will be able to direct you to one of the free plots avaliable at the top of the Hexagon.
Page 4 of 9
Government ------------------- Fellowship is run by a series of Ministers and a pair of Diarchs. One diarch is elected while the other is permanent. To be able to partake in elections and government functions you must become a "Fellow" which means...
Page 5 of 9
Government Cont. ------------------- ... that you have lived within Fellowship for three weeks and have been accepted by all current Fellows.
Page 6 of 9
Laws & Rules ------------------- 1. Do not kill unless given permission or if you're attacking a known offender of the law. 2. Do not steal. 3. Do not assault others.
Page 7 of 9
Laws & Rules Cont. ------------------- 4. Do not trespass within private property of others without permission. 5. Do not grief others with blocks, fire, words, etc. Please do not use obscenities in excess.
Page 8 of 9
Rights ------------------- 1. All individuals have a right to a home. 2. All individuals have a right to private property. 3. All individuals have the right to basic reinforcement mats.
Page 9 of 9
Rights Cont. ------------------- 4. All individuals have the right to appeal legal matters to the Diarchs and the Ministry.
