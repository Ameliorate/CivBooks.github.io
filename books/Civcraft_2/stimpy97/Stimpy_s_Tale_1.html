There once was a boy named Stimpy. He was a lonely peasant that lived in a little house outside of Orion. He usually spent his days harvesting carrots and feeding cows. He didn't usually travel into the big city of Orion because it was much too busy.
Page 3 of 48
The city was scary to him, with all the men who wandered about in their weird glowing armor. These men would hassle Stimpy for being a wee farmer boy. The armored men had the best gear while poor Stimpy had nothing but his carrots and stone tools.
Page 4 of 48
Stimpy didn't always want to be a farmer. At a young age Stimpy had been curious about the warrior lifestyle, but he never got the opportunity to train to fight.
Page 5 of 48
He was sent off to work the field by his displeased masters, Theodius and Norami, just for being in the way. However, being a farmer boy did have its perks.
Page 6 of 48
He enjoyed the peace and quiet, and he also liked that he did not have to worry about learning big city things. He had a pretty good life. That is... up until weird things started happening.

The morning started like any other. The young farmer boy ate some bread, milked the pigs, and went about the daily task of bringing a load of carrots into the bustling town of Orion. He noticed nothing.
