Iria Region Nether Factory Agreement ------------------- Hjaltland shall have the option of investing into Iria's nether factory by providing the outstanding endstone and emerald requirements in return for an ownership stake in the Iria factory.
Page 2 of 6
Terms: The stake will be proportional to what Hjaltland invests. Hjaltland will be entitled to a cut of profits from factory commercialization, and will have input on the factory name, design and access policy.
Page 3 of 6
If the leadership of Iria so decides, he may purchase the Hjaltland ownership for an agreed upon price related to the current cost of a stack of Endstone and Emeralds.
