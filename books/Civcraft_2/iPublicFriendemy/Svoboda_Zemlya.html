What is the Order of the Proletariate? The Order would be a group of 6 permenant citizens of Senntisten. Within this group, at least 2 members must have been a citizen for over 1 month. This ensures that there are multiple members who understand the
Page 20 of 38
basics of CivCraft. The oldest member is considered the Proletarian leader. The Order will decide on laws and proposals put forth by the people of Senntisten. The people will vote for their leader, except for three
Page 21 of 38
positions. These positions are to be held by the King, SeldomShock, and two citizens of his choosing. Should he or his choices step down, be pearled, leave, or otherwise become unable to serve, they will be replaced by the new king and/or new choices.
Page 22 of 38
What will society revolve around? Similar to in Leningrad, society will revolve around working for the greater good of Senntisten. But, unlike Leningrad, the greater good will include the freedom and equality of the proletariate.
Page 23 of 38
The people of Senntisten will be entitled to 3 basic rights: 1) Right to free speech, press and religion. 2) Right to personal property and whatever built on it. 3) Right to citizenship to the great city of Senntisten.
