THE BEGINNING TESTAMENT The Kingdom His holiness 8BitChap was first to step foot on the ground, after the Elder Mods created the New World. His step gave birth to life, and he spread the glory of nature and of peace

throughout the lands of CivCraft. Where he stepped, roses sprouted and grasses flourished. As he passed by sheep, they grew fur thicker than any of their flock. As he passed by cows, their milk was richer and more abundant than all those in the

largest of herds. 8BitChap brought the gifts of life to all four quadrants of the world. He wandered the lands, spreading his message of goodness to all those who would listen. Newfriends were given a breath of life with his every movement, and
Page 6 of 33
they would flock to him seeking his great wisdom. One day, a newfriend came to his holiness, asking for how he should go about running his kingdom. "Oh great 8BitChap," the newfriend said as he bowed down, "how might I lead to please

THE END TESTAMENT 8BitChap stood silent in the End. He showed no fear nor no regret. He had been a martyr for the good of all, and he had done what the Elder Mods had sent him to do. His holiness did not concern himself with his freedom,
Page 29 of 33
but instead with the morality of his brothers trapped with him. Griefers, thieves, and innocently pearled men alike gathered around as he taught the good word. His path that followed him was lined with flowers and crops, all of which fed the
Page 30 of 33
hungry prisoners of the dark and bleak End. 8BitChap taught all those who would pass by. He spoke of his travels across the world, and of his tenets to a proper life. He blessed the forgotten, and gave away all his wealth
