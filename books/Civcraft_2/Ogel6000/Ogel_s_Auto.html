I joined Civcraft the day of the map reset. I did play on the server a few hours before the reset, however, and in this time visited Rift, Constantinople, 0,0, and a couple of others which I am unable to name.

When 2.0 was launched, I was lucky enough to make it online for day 1. I spent the next few days wandering around without settling. I visited Commonwealth the day it was founded, along with very early Holy Tree. I probably visited others as well, though

I do not remember exactly which. I joined the True Neutral City, or the collective camp of Aurora, Fellowship, and Bryn while the three were seeking locations to settle. I was among the first to build a house in Aurora. Maybe the third.
