Preamble This constitution is for the people of Source and environs. All those under the banner of DEoS will coher to any and all standards, laws, and principles found within. The foremost purpose of this document is to provide the framework and

Expectations of Citizens A citizen is defined as anyone living in Source proper, and any cities, neighborhoods, or other facilities under the banner of DEoS. All citizens are expected to pay taxes, aid the empire if called upon,
Page 6 of 11
vote on laws, and to maintain their own property. If the Emperor or his assisstants cannot be found, citizens are still required to follow established laws. Citizens will also act as juries in court cases and can propose legislation.
Page 7 of 11
Government Structure The government of DEoS is headed by the Emperor. There are two under-emperors, who will be called Caesars. The Emperor may appoint and remove them at will. The Emperor and the Caesars may propose laws, but the Emperor
Page 8 of 11
has the final say. An Imperial Veto can be repealed by 2/3 of the citizen body. All citizens have a single vote, the Emperor and Caesars being no exception. All are expected to follow the laws, including the Emperor and Caesars. If neither the Emperor or
Page 9 of 11
the Caesars are contactable, then DEoS will be run by the citizens as a complete Democracy until their return. The same rules apply during this period, unless the citizens change the laws.
Page 10 of 11
Rights of Citizens All citizens have the right to vote, assemble, speak freely, have a fair trial, protection from griefers, and to do as they wish, so long as it does not infringe upon the rights of other citizens.
Page 11 of 11
Crime and Punishment If a citizen is found to be breaking a law of DEoS, they will can or will be fined, pearled, forced to repair damage, mine a set quota of an ore, have property revoked, or have citizenship revoked.
