Alexandria was a city in the (+,-) Quadrant. It was a fair size, consisting of about 12 houses and various public buildings, most notably a large sand/sandstone pyramid outside the city walls. Said walls were made of unreinforced

sandstone. As of recently, Alexandria is inactive and griefed. There are signs around the city telling of an Exodus to the south, and a vague account of the fall of the city. Many of the signs end with the words “Pharoah protect us”
