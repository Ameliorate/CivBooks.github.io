__Chapter 1__ Mooshroom The Mooshroom is, most likely, one of the more... crazy animals Minecraft has to offer. With big, black eyes, red and white hide, and toadstools growing on their back, these are not a common animal, and are found in

only one biome, the Mushroom Biome. They prefer to be near mushrooms, be it brown or white, and are sometimes found naturally spawned on the huge fungus called the Huge Mushroom.

__Chapter 2__ Characteristics of the Mooshroom Mooshrooms have similarities to normal cows, such as they can be milked with buckets to get milk, they drop leather and raw beef when killed, and still breed with wheat bunches.
Page 4 of 16
But Mooshrooms are also very different from the common domesticated cow, with unnatural characteristics one is sure to enjoy, such as they are a source of infinite food, the mushroom stew, which one can milk with a wooden bowl. Second, they can be sheared
Page 5 of 16
and transformed into a normal cow, dropping red fungi, but unfortunately, this change cannot be reversed. They prefer their homeland grass, Mycelium, which is a dull pink-purple color in contrast to our bright green normal grass.

__Chapter 3__ Owning a Mooshroom If you plan to own a mooshroom (or two), be sure to have these essential thngs listed. Mycelium- Mycelium is essential for mooshroooms, and one cannot lve without it.

A Decent Size Pen- Don't cram your mooshrooms! Be sure to at least have a 5x5x3 pen at the ready for your new animal. Fungi- Don't leave your pen floor barren-add some fungi to it!

__Chapter 4__ The Habitat of the Mooshroom The Mooshroom is found in only one biome, the Mushroom Biome, where instead of tall grass grows small fungi, instead of grass grows the strange Mycelium, and instead of trees grows the Huge Mushroom.
