Introduction ----------------This book records the location of books within a standard Terminus Literary Vault. All vaults will have the same books organized in the same manner. Use this as your guide to create new ones.
Page 3 of 7
Floors ----------------Each vault will have multiple floors, usually descending into the ground. The first floor is designated as Floor 1, the one below it Floor 2, and so on, until reaching bedrock.
Page 4 of 7
Chests ----------------Each floor of a Terminus Literary Valut will have 27 chests, organized into 3 rows and 9 columns. The columns should be marked A-I and the rows 1-3.
Page 5 of 7
Book Designations ----------------The reason we have such strict guidlines for Terminus Literary Vault construction is to facilitate the creation of duplicate vaults. This will help ensure that if one vault gets destroyed, others will have backups of
