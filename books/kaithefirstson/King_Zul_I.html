Chapter 3: The Discovery of the Diamond Vein. The diamond ore that was exposed during the battle with Meiter was part of the largest diamont vein ever discovered in all of the known realm. Zulstead became the most wealthy city in
Page 29 of 46
the South, and possibly the entire known world. The vein was scouted before any mining activity was sanctioned and nearly 2,000 diamonds were visible in the central vein with around 400 spread out across 3 seperate branches, and smaller sub branches.

Zul chartered a royal diamond mining company with a board of directors, their mission being to mine the diamonds but also to control the supply rate as to avoid destabilizing the entire economy of the realm. The diamond mines of King Zul proved to be the
Page 31 of 46
richest ever discovered, bringing incalculable wealth into the Kingdom of Zul. Anything that could be bought, was sold in Zul's bazaars. Weapons, materials, books, slaves, mercenaries, and entertainment were all sold in the markets. The city was wealthy

beyond all other cities in the region, perhaps the entire land itself. The wealth was such that the poorest citizen in Zul's kingdom was richer than all of the richest citizens in the next 5 cities combined. The diamond mines allowed Zulstead to multiply
Page 33 of 46
