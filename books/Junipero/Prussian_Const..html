2nd Rank: Member of Parliament Members of Parliament are the Government of Prussia. Members without a specific role other than voting on state affairs are referred to as "Senator", all others are "Minister". People
Page 15 of 50
can be multiple Ministers at once, also There can be Ministries without a Minister, if there is noone willing and capable to do it. The Parliament will be elected by the people upon the foundation of the state. After this, the Ministers
Page 16 of 50
nominate their successors. If more than half of the Citiziens want that a Senator or Minister resigns, he has to do so. If this happens, or a Minister or Senator resigns without nominating a Successor, the Parliament votes on
Page 17 of 50

Section 2.1: Diplomacy with Individuals Everyone is welcome in every Prussian Settlement. There are however certain rules that have to be respected. A trespasser will be Pearled if:
Page 33 of 50
-He attempts to destroy or grief property of Prussia or that of one of its Citiziens -He walks into private areas and doesn't make an attempt to leave after being told twice.

-He attacks a Prussian anywhere or a peaceful Visitor on Prussian Ground -He clearly states in Mumble, ingame chat, reddit or via any other media, that he intends to do one of the above

Section 2.2 Diplomacy with States Prussia respects and accepts every foreign Law on the Server, as long as it follows the Non-Aggression- Principle. Every State has the possibility to build an Embassy in the Prussian Capital.
Page 42 of 50
If an Individual acts on behalf of a State or Group while breaking Prussian Law, the Parliament can decide to declare a War against this State or Group. The Parliament has to check if the malicious actions were really ordered by a government before
Page 43 of 50
declaring a war. Prisoners of war are arbitrated personally, depending on the war-crimes they committed and their ranks in the State. In Times of War or Danger every Prussian has the duty to wear his best armor at all times. In times
