Welcome, traveller, to your guide to the world! Within, you'll find information of the capitals of the countries of the world! While not a perfect text, with this at your side, you should be able to navigate any country with ease.§0 §0 §0Now, let's get started!

--==NORLUND==--§0 §0A nation far to the north, Norlund is a true parliamentary monarchy, with a prime minister and king both acting as heads of state. It too has adopted a currency, the krun, and has a booming carrot industry, with fields upon fields of them.

--==Carbon==--§0 §0Carbon is, by far, the most wide-reaching nation in the world, with a colony on almost every landmass. It is a constitutional monarchy. Its capitol shares the name, just tacking "City of " to the front. It is a hodgepodge of buildings, with

--==The Commune==-- The Commune is a small place based on the river. It is anarcho-communist, and as such has no state or economy. It is run by a pure democracy. The capitol which shares its name is not technologically advanced, and there isn't much to
