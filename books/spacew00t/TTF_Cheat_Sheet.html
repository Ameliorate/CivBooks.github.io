Terminus Book Identifier ----------------When you are ready to sign your book, get a TBI from the current Terminus Head Librarians (currently Spacew00t and OogalaBoogala). If they are not online, then leave it blank until you get the TBI.

Multi-Volume Works ----------------When signing the book, add v.1 to the end of its title for first volumes, v.2 for second volumes, v.3 for third volumes, and so on.
