The Hungry Traveler and The Kind Farmer During a long travel across the map, there was a traveler who began to starve from the few thousands blocks he had covered. Suddenly he came across a vast wheat field.
Page 9 of 26
In front there was a sign that read, “Public use, please replant.” “Perfect,” thought the nearly starved traveler, “What a kind farmer to plant food for everyone, and just in time.”
Page 10 of 26
He took all of the wheat, and seeing no one was around decided he would not waste time replanting. So leaving the field barren he went off and arrived at his destination safely with plenty of food to spare.
Page 11 of 26
Upon logging and seeing his field void of crop, the farmer checked his snitches and ran to the nearest town in which he also supplied food for to warn them of a crop thief. As he arrived he saw the man who took without replanting.
Page 12 of 26
He alerted the guards and they immediately pearled him. The farmer said to himself as he watched, “The time you saved, decidedly cost you more time than it takes to replant for others.”

Uguu and the Newfriend A member of Uguu and a Newfriend were travelling together, the man from Uguu prancing along in his fine prot and carrying only food and a fancy sword, the Newfriend in iron and gold mob armor trudging carrying stone and a pick.
