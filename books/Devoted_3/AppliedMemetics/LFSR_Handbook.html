LFSR: Linear Feedback Shift-Register It is formed from a long chain of components (flip-flops IRL, Repeaters IG) that hold a state for a clock cycle, and a XOR gate that taps into specific elements (Repeaters) to randomize output.

(In the implementation I use of the LSFR, the XOR gate provides 3 ticks of delay, which is why a repeater is necessary after it, so that total delay = 4 ticks which keeps the clock rates stable as the XOR gate also acts as an element)
