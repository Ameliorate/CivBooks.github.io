AVE MARIA INTRODUCTION Prayer to saints is often a confusing subject to heretics, and so it need not be confused with idol worship. We do not worship the Mother of God, but we do venerate her.
Page 11 of 20
The structure of the prayer, as with all prayers to the saints, is to acknowledge who they are, then petition for their prayers. In the Ave Maria, we quote scripture for the first stanza, recognize her in the first line of the second stanza, and then
