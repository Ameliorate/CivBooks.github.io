§1MemeMana§r You get Mana every day for logging in regularly. This is used to maintain an Exile Pearl and is very valuable to some players. Selling your Mana can be a good way to get some starting resources.

§1Namelayer§r Manage groups! Type "/nljg ! !" without quotes for global chat and "/g !" to chat in the group. Type "/nl" to create and join groups. Many other features use groups – lock your doors so only your allies can enter with Citadel! Forge friendships!
