Page 2 of 14
Fade in. Ext, Mount Augustan Street. Siksta is walking alone, down the dark streets. A dark figure appears in the gloom of a nearby building.
Page 3 of 14
PapaPound: Siksta. Siksta stops walking Siksta: (.inner monologue.) It was a voice from the past, a voice I had hoped would stay there. aloud Papa_Pound. What brings you to Mount Augusta this time of night?
Page 4 of 14
PapaPound: I like the air here. Somewhat... freer. Siksta: Cut the crap Papa. What do you want? PapaPound: You know what I want.
Page 5 of 14
Siksta: I gave that life up. I don't do that anymore. PapaPound: You were the best. You think I could go to Chrischrispie for this? You think I would go to anyone but the best for this job?
Page 6 of 14
Siksta: I GAVE IT UP. I don't write porn anymore, not after... PapaPound: whisper Papa_Pounded was a masterpiece Siksta: STOP. I don't want to talk about it.
Page 7 of 14
PapaPound: I'm not asking you to. I'm asking you to write something new. One last book, Siksta. One last chance to make a difference. Siksta: One last run at the game... PapaPound: Exactly.
Page 8 of 14
Siksta: And what's the prize? PapaPound: It's not the prize you should worry about Siksta. It's the cost of failure. Papa walks out from the shadows, flicks a cigarette into the street.
Page 9 of 14
His breastplate is blazoned with Noxian emblems PapaPound: The fate of the server hangs in the balance. Siksta: Who are the subjects, Papa?
Page 10 of 14
PapaPound: Sintralin and Shadedjon. Nox and Eden. World Police and HCF. The whole server needs this, Siksta. We need you. Siksta steps forward, standing eye-to-eye with Papa.
Page 11 of 14
Siksta: I'll write your damned book. Siksta turns and continues walking down the path, Papa leans back against the building and lights another cigarette. End scene.
