I. Time and You Time brings entropy, and with it: destruction. As mountains erode, as lakes lose their source waters, as all things become no more, so is the fate of all people.

Take comfort in this. Every soul to ever live has known its own death. It is the most common thing in the universe. We are lucky to die, for it means that we are alive.
