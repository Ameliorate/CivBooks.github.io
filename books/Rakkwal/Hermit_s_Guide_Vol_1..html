Travelling Having to travel is usually inevitable if you want to take advantage of luxories that cannot be obtained through this playstyle. Many cities have a public resource pool, from Pickaxe factories
Page 12 of 19
to cheaply obtainable tools through shops. Gaining access to public factories and worspaces are usually as simple as messaging a member within the town. While having to speak to someone may be unfortunate, it is worth it. When you are
Page 13 of 19
travelling, try to only carry the minimum. A minecart and a spare, a boat, and for long journeys a bed. Try not to stand out by being heavily geared, but don't go anywhere unarmed or naked. In both cases, you will attract unwanted attention.

Security For monitoring purposes, you will usually have the advantage. You likely wont have a large claim, and therefore wont have to create a very complex snitch grid. Because you are less likely to use noteblock
Page 15 of 19
style snitches, the majority of your network will be Jukeboxes. Each monitors a 3d cube with a functional radius of 11 blocks, making it easy to secure a large area. Jukeboxes snitches can be set up with CastleGates/Redstone to create complex

traps. After placing a Juke, use command "/jaToggleLevers 1" to enable lever output. The adjacent block a lever is placed will determine the input required to toggle the lever. Levers are only toggled by players not on the snitch group.

Living alone isn't easy and it's not for anyone with delusions of relevancy. You will have more fun with a group. Hermits don't need to care for WP politics, they rarely affect us. So if you have an itch for PVP, don't kid yourself with living quietly.
