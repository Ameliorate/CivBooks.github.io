This is a story that spans a period of 48 hours, beginning on the Fourth of July, or the 412th day of the second age of Civ. The events begin in the city of Orion, after what appeared to be a simple raid.

Reinforced stone was covering the major roads, but the attack itself was not too massive, all things considered. The real danger arose during the cleanup efforts. While the citizens, instructed by their acting mayor Itaqi, were removing the stone,

the true attack was sprung. Fifteen men, who later identified as the Butter Brigade stormed the city, in order to claim the pearl of it's leaders. The battle was swift, as there were only a handful of citizens nearby. Sadly, though Itaqi was pearled.

The invading force, revelling in their victory, loudly stated their lofty goals of pearling the leaders of every major city. At this, it was time the allegorical trumpet to be blown. The warriors of the Minus- Minus quadrant were summoned to defend

their home. A number of early responers managed to get to Orion fairly quickly. They were led by Shamrock Jones of the Metro. Though they eventually fell, the defenders managed to delay the marauders long enough for a true army to emerge.

While the army of the North assembled, Orion was ravaged. Obsidian and Stone covered the major streets, while lava rained from the sky. In the heart of the city, a vault of over twelve layers was built to hold those already captured.
