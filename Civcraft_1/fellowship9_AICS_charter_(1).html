The council will convene every two months to elect a new secretary general. Anyone accused of war crimes/crimes against civcraft will be tried in the International Court of Justice. This does not mean small time criminals or griefers.

Any nation state found trading with a state on which the council has declared an embargo will be banned from the council until they have a new leader. no member state is to interact with a state which has been banned from the council for any reason.
